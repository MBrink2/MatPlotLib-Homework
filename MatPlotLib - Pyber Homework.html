Observations¶

Less, more expensive rides are taken in rural areas while Urban areas see more frequent rides at lower costs.

While there are more rides taken overall in Urban areas, Suburban areas contribute more than their fare share of fare compared to rides taken. This is due to an average higher fare in suburban areas compared to urban areas.

Rural areas contribute the least to overall breakdown of fare and rides despite highest overall costs.
